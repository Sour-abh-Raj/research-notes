# Thoughts

This section is a notebook for thoughts — observations, proto-theories, and research agendas worth preserving before they are either abandoned or developed into something more formal.

Most entries here are not yet proposals. They are observations formalized: a noticed mechanism, a candidate question, a possible empirical path. The value of writing them down is that ideas die when nobody records them.

Each entry follows a consistent structure:

- **Core Observation** — what was noticed, stated precisely
- **Proposed Mechanism** — how it might work
- **Key Insight** — the potentially novel part
- **Candidate Constructs** — the theoretical vocabulary that might apply
- **Research Questions and Propositions** — the formal research agenda, preserved as-is for later use
- **Connections** — how this thought relates to others in this notebook
